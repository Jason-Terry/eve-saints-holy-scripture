# PvE Doctrine

## What We Fly and Why

- **Missiles and Drones**
- **Passive armor tank on DPS ships.**
- **Armor logi for cruiser fleets.** Our cruisers are armor tanked, so they need armor reps.
- **Shield logi for battleship fleets.** Ravens and Drakes are shield tanked — they're the big investments, they get the reps.

See [[Fleet Dynamics]] for the full reasoning behind these choices.

## Golden Rules For A Good Fleet

1. **Fly the role the fleet needs, at the highest tier you can.**
2. **Always carry EC-300 ECM drones if you can to counter tackle.**
3. **Armor logi for cruiser fleets. Shield logi for battleship fleets.**
4. **Scout on the hole.**
5. **FC's should run a command burst.**
