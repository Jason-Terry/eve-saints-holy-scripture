## Scenarios

| Situation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **Hostile on hole** | Scout tackles, fleet warps at optimal |
| **Tackled on site** | Fleet aligns, tackle burns to hostile, fight or scatter |

## Golden Rules For Home Defense

1. **Scout on the hole at all times.**
2. **Hostiles called — DPS warps to hole at optimal, tackle warps to zero.**
3. **Don't jump through the hole.**
4. **Commit to the FC's call. We win or die together.**
5. **Fly the role the fleet needs, at the highest tier you can.**
6. **Always carry EC-300 ECM drones.**
7. **Logi priority — keep the big ships alive.**
