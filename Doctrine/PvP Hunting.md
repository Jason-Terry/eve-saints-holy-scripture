# PvP Hunting

## What We Fly and Why

- **Shield fleet.** Hurricanes, Drakes, Ravens, Osprey logi — one tank type, one logi type.
- **Hurricanes brawl, missiles stay at range.** Canes burn in and hold the fight, missile ships apply DPS from optimal.
- **Scout finds the target, FC moves the fleet.** Stratios or Astero scouts the chain cloaked, gets tackle, fleet jumps in.
- **Newbros bring tackle or range DPS.** Atron for tackle, Corax or Caracal for missiles.

See [[Fleet Dynamics]] for the full reasoning behind these choices.

**Always at least one of every role before you undock.** Extra bodies fill DPS or tackle.

## Golden Rules For Hunting

1. **Scout finds the target, FC moves the fleet.**
2. **Fleet holds cloak until FC calls break.**
3. **At least one of every role before you undock.**
4. **Hurricanes burn in, missiles stay at range.**
5. **FC commits to saving the scout. When tackle is called, the fleet is coming.**
6. **Commit to the FC's call. We win or die together.**
7. **Always carry EC-300 ECM drones.**
8. **Don't chase through holes you haven't scouted.**
